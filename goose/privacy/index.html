Privacy Policy — GOOSE

Last updated: April 16, 2026

GOOSE is built and operated by AIRGATE LLC ("we," "our," or "us").

GOOSE is a social app-blocking app that lets groups block distracting apps together during shared sessions. This Privacy Policy explains what data GOOSE collects, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect and Why

Account and Authentication

When you sign in with Apple, we receive information needed to authenticate your account, such as an Apple-provided identifier and, if available, your display name. Depending on your Sign in with Apple settings, Apple may also provide an email address or a private relay email address.

We use this information to create and maintain your GOOSE account. Authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication, a service operated by Google.

Profile Information

We store a display name and an emoji associated with your account. This information is visible to other participants when you join a session.

Session Data

When you create or join a session, we store session-related information such as session name, session timing, participant membership, and session settings. This data is necessary for the app to function and is stored in Firebase Realtime Database, a service operated by Google.

Presence and Feed Data

During a session, we store your blocking status, join and leave times, and session feed events such as when participants join, leave, or exit early. This data is used to provide the real-time session experience.

Session-related information, including participant names, emoji, join and leave status, and feed events, is visible to other participants in the same session. This visibility is a core feature of the app.

Bluetooth

GOOSE uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) locally on-device to help detect nearby sessions. BLE data is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Screen Time and App Blocking

GOOSE uses Apple's Screen Time-related system APIs to block apps on your device during sessions. We do not collect, store, or transmit the list of apps you use, your Screen Time usage history, or your Screen Time settings. Blocking runs locally on your device.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not use third-party analytics or third-party crash-reporting SDKs in the current build. We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers. Our service providers may process technical information required to operate authentication and database services.

Third-Party Services

GOOSE currently uses the following third-party services:

  • Sign in with Apple (Apple) — used to sign you in. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — used to authenticate your account. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Realtime Database (Google) — used to store account and session-related data needed for the app to function.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising or behavioral targeting.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain account, profile, session, presence, and feed data for as long as needed to operate GOOSE and support your account, unless you request deletion or we no longer need the data.

For the current beta, you can request account and associated data deletion by contacting us at contact@airgateapp.com.

Children's Privacy

GOOSE is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, we will delete that information.

Future family-oriented features, if added, may require updated parental consent and privacy disclosures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@airgateapp.com.
