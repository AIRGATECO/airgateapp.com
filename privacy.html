Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

AIRGATE LLC ("we", "our", or "us") built Proxitime as a privacy-first application. This Privacy Policy explains how Proxitime handles your information.

Our Core Principle

Proxitime is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any server. There are no user accounts, no cloud services, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

What Proxitime Uses

Proxitime uses the following device capabilities to function:

  • Bluetooth — to discover and connect with nearby devices running Proxitime. Bluetooth data is exchanged directly between devices and is never sent to any server.
  • Ultra-Wideband (UWB) — to measure precise distance between connected devices. UWB ranging data stays on your device.
  • Multipeer Connectivity — to exchange connection tokens between nearby devices over local Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. This communication is device-to-device only.
  • Screen Time / Family Controls — to block selected apps when you are near a connected partner. App blocking rules are stored locally on your device.

Data We Collect

None. Proxitime does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or any other data. We have no servers and no databases.

Data Shared Between Devices

When you connect with another Proxitime user, the following is exchanged directly between your devices over encrypted local connections:

  • A randomly generated peer identifier (not tied to your Apple ID or any personal information)
  • A display name you choose
  • UWB session tokens (used for distance measurement, valid only for the current session)

This data is never transmitted to AIRGATE or any third party. It exists only on the connected devices and is discarded when sessions end.

Location Data

Proxitime does not use GPS, cellular location, or any location services. It measures only the relative distance between two devices using UWB and Bluetooth signal strength. No location data is collected, stored, or shared.

Third-Party Services

Proxitime does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Proxitime does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
