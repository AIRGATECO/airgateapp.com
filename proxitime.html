What is Proxitime?

Proxitime is a proximity-aware app blocker that helps you be present with the people who matter most. When you're physically near your partner or a close friend, Proxitime automatically blocks distracting apps on your phone — and unlocks them when you move apart. Using Ultra-Wideband technology for sub-meter accuracy, it knows exactly when you're together without tracking your location.

How It Works

1

Connect

Pair with your partner via Bluetooth. One tap to link your devices.

2

Choose

Select which apps to block when you're together. Fully customizable per person.

3

Be Present

When you're nearby, selected apps are automatically blocked. No willpower needed.

4

Move Apart

Walk away and your apps unlock. Simple, automatic, and seamless.

Key Features

Ultra-Wideband Precision

Sub-meter distance accuracy on supported iPhones for reliable proximity detection.

Bluetooth Fallback

Works on all iPhones with Bluetooth, even without UWB hardware.

Per-Partner Rules

Customize which apps to block for each person you connect with.

Real-Time Distance

See exactly how far away your partner is with a live distance display.

Works in Background

Runs quietly in the background. No need to keep the app open.

Privacy-First

No location tracking, no servers, no accounts. Everything stays on your device.
